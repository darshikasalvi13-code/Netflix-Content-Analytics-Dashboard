# Netflix Content Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Netflix's movies and TV shows dataset to uncover content trends, genre popularity, ratings distribution, and global content availability.

An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to provide business insights and enable dynamic filtering of Netflix content.

---

## Problem Statement
Netflix offers thousands of movies and TV shows across different countries and genres. The objective of this project is to analyze Netflix content and identify patterns in:

- Content distribution (Movies vs TV Shows)
- Genre popularity
- Content ratings
- Growth of Netflix content over time
- Global content availability

---

## Objectives
- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing using Python.
- Conduct Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
- Create meaningful business insights.
- Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

## Dataset Information
- Dataset: Netflix Titles Dataset
- Total Records: 8,711
- Source: Kaggle

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Python

- Seaborn

## Data Cleaning Performed
- Handled missing values
- Converted date columns into datetime format
- Created new features:
  - year_added
  - month_added
  - content_age
  - duration_category
- Removed duplicates and corrected data types

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Movies dominate Netflix
- Movies account for approximately 70% of total content.
- TV Shows account for approximately 30%.

### 2. Rapid growth after 2016
- Netflix significantly expanded its content library between 2016 and 2020.

### 3. Popular Ratings
- TV-MA and TV-14 are the most common content ratings.

### 4. Genre Trends
- International Movies and Documentaries are among the most popular genres.

### 5. Global Distribution
- The United States contributes the highest number of titles.

---

## Key Business Insights

- Movies constitute approximately 70% of Netflix content.
- Netflix experienced rapid content growth after 2016.
- TV-MA and TV-14 dominate ratings.
- The United States contributes the highest amount of content.
- International Movies and Documentaries are among the most popular genres.
